Diary of a Wimpy Kid



Author: Jeff Kinney
Date of publish: 1 April 2007

ABOUT:
"Diary of a Wimpy Kid" is a popular children's book series written by Jeff Kinney. The first book in the series was published in 2007 and follows the misadventures of middle school student Greg Heffley as he navigates the challenges of growing up, including bullying, peer pressure, and family problems. The series has been praised for its relatable characters, engaging storytelling, and humorous illustrations. It has become a global phenomenon, with over 250 million copies sold worldwide and several film adaptations.

SUMMARY:
"Diary of a Wimpy Kid" is a popular children's book series written by American author Jeff Kinney. The series follows the misadventures of middle school student Greg Heffley, who narrates the stories through his journal.

In the first book, which was published in 2007, Greg starts writing in his diary on the first day of middle school. He is determined to become popular and believes that a diary will help him achieve that goal. However, his plans quickly go awry as he struggles to navigate the challenges of middle school life, including bullying, peer pressure, and family problems.

Throughout the book, Greg's diary entries are accompanied by illustrations that add to the humor and appeal of the story. The book has been praised for its relatable characters and situations, as well as its ability to engage reluctant readers.

As the series progresses, Greg continues to write about his experiences in middle school and beyond, including his adventures during summer vacation, his attempts to impress a girl, and his struggles with his older brother. The series has become a global phenomenon, with over 250 million copies sold worldwide, and has been adapted into several films.

Overall, "Diary of a Wimpy Kid" is a humorous and engaging book series that captures the challenges and anxieties of growing up, making it a popular choice among young readers.
