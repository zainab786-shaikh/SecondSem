How the Earth got its Beauty



Author: Sudha Murthy
Date of publish: July 2021

ABOUT:
"How the Earth Got Its Beauty" is a children's book written by Sudha Murthy and illustrated by Priyankar Gupta. It tells the story of how the earth was created and became a place of beauty and wonder through the cooperation of different elements of nature such as the sun, wind, rain, and trees. The book emphasizes the importance of teamwork and celebrates the beauty and diversity of the natural world. It is aimed at children aged 8-12 years and is a great addition to any child's library. The book is beautifully illustrated and the text is simple and easy to read, making it accessible to young readers. It is a delightful book that will inspire children to appreciate the natural world and to work together to create something beautiful.

SUMMARY:

"How the Earth Got Its Beauty" is a beautifully illustrated children's book that tells the story of how the earth was created and how it became a place of beauty and wonder. The book is written by Sudha Murthy, an Indian author and philanthropist, and is illustrated by Priyankar Gupta.

The book starts with a barren and lifeless earth, with no colors or beauty to be found anywhere. The different elements of nature, such as the sun, wind, rain, and trees, decide to work together to create something beautiful. The sun provides the warmth and light, the wind brings the seeds of plants and flowers, the rain brings the water, and the trees provide the shelter and the framework for the new world to grow.

As the different elements of nature work together, they create a variety of landscapes, from mountains to oceans, forests to deserts, and everything in between. The book takes the reader on a journey through the different landscapes of the earth, exploring their beauty and diversity.

Sudha Murthy emphasizes the importance of teamwork and cooperation throughout the book. The different elements of nature all have their own unique qualities, but they need to work together to create something truly beautiful. The book also celebrates the beauty and diversity of the natural world and encourages readers to appreciate and protect the environment around them.

The illustrations in the book are stunning and bring the different landscapes of the earth to life. Priyankar Gupta's illustrations are colorful and detailed, and they capture the beauty and diversity of the natural world. The text is simple and easy to read, making the book accessible to young readers.

Overall, "How the Earth Got Its Beauty" is a wonderful book that will inspire children to appreciate the natural world and to work together to create something beautiful. The book encourages readers to be curious and explore the world around them, and it celebrates the beauty and diversity of the natural world. It is a great addition to any child's library and will be enjoyed by children and adults alike.
