Tales of Punjab



Author: "Folktales of Punjab" by Flora Annie Steel
 Date of publish: 25 may 2016

ABOUT:The book contains a collection of traditional folktales and legends from the Punjab region of India and Pakistan. The tales are divided into categories such as "Animal Tales," "Ghost Stories," "Religious Legends," and "Love Stories," and they provide a glimpse into the culture, traditions, and beliefs of the people of Punjab.

SUMMARY:The book contains a collection of traditional folktales and legends from the Punjab region of India and Pakistan, divided into categories such as "Animal Tales," "Ghost Stories," "Religious Legends," and "Love Stories." The tales provide insight into the culture, traditions, and beliefs of the people of Punjab and are told in a simple and engaging style. The book has been reprinted several times and remains a popular choice for those interested in Punjabi folklore and literature.
