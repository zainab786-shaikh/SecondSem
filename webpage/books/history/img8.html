Caraneries and cage birds



Author: Shashi Tharoor
 Date of publish: 25 may 2016

ABOUT:"Caraneries and cage bird" by Toby Wilkinson is a highly regarded book that provides a comprehensive account of ancient Egypt from the pre-dynastic period to the end of the New Kingdom. The book explores various aspects of Egyptian history, including politics, relig ion, society, art, and architecture, and provides insights into the daily lives of the ancient Egyptians..

SUMMARY:Caraneries and cage birds The book draws on a wide range of sources, including archaeological evidence, hieroglyphic inscriptions, and ancient texts, to provide a detailed and engaging narrative of Egypt's history. It discusses the major figures and e vents of each period, including the reigns of famous pharaohs such as Tutankhamun and Ramses II.
