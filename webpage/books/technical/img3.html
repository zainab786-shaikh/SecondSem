Science



Author: Abigail Beall and Jack Challoner
Date of publish: 30 June 2016

ABOUT:
"It is well-researched and has child-friendly content The various topics improve knowledge about the world around us and beyond The content will cater to curious minds and expand their horizon The book comprises a glossary of new and difficult terms It is an excellent selection for gifting and school libraries".

SUMMARY:
"What is the Theory of Evolution? How do chemical reactions occur? Why is the human eye most receptive to only three colors? This encyclopedia on science will answer these and more hows and whys for you. Learning is made simpler with well-labelled diagrams and an extensive glossary of difficult words. Bonus: the engrossing book comes loaded with Isn’t It Amazing—a section of fun facts to keep you glued for more. This encyclopedia is a must-read for all children!"
