Verity



Author: Colleen Hoover
Date of publish: 7 December 2018

ABOUT:
"Verity" is a psychological thriller novel written by Colleen Hoover, published in 2018. The book follows Lowen Ashleigh, a struggling writer who is hired by bestselling author Verity Crawford to finish writing the remaining books in her popular book series. As Lowen begins to sort through Verity's notes and research, she discovers a hidden manuscript that reveals shocking truths about Verity and her family. As Lowen delves deeper into the manuscript, she becomes increasingly obsessed and finds herself drawn to Verity's husband, Jeremy, despite the strain that Verity's accident has placed on their marriage. The book explores themes of obsession, betrayal, and the darker side of human nature. It was a New York Times bestseller and has been optioned for a television series.

SUMMARY:
"Verity" is a psychological thriller novel written by Colleen Hoover. The book was published in 2018 and quickly gained popularity due to its intense plot, well-drawn characters, and dark themes.

The story follows Lowen Ashleigh, a struggling writer who is hired by bestselling author Verity Crawford to finish writing the remaining books in her popular book series. Verity has been in a tragic accident and is unable to complete the books herself. As Lowen begins to sort through Verity's notes and research for the series, she discovers a hidden manuscript in Verity's office. The manuscript is an autobiographical account of Verity's life, and the shocking truths that it reveals about Verity and her family begin to unravel as Lowen becomes increasingly obsessed with it.

As the story progresses, Lowen finds herself drawn to Verity's husband, Jeremy, despite his troubled past and the strain that Verity's accident has placed on their marriage. As Lowen delves deeper into Verity's manuscript, she realizes that there may be more to Verity's accident than meets the eye, and that the lives of everyone involved may be in danger.

The narrative is divided into two parts, the present day and Verity's manuscript. The manuscript provides a glimpse into Verity's past, revealing her darkest secrets and creating a sense of unease and uncertainty in the reader's mind. The present-day narrative focuses on Lowen's increasing obsession with Verity's manuscript, and her growing feelings for Jeremy.

The book's themes include obsession, betrayal, and the darker side of human nature. Hoover's writing style is gripping, and her ability to create tension and suspense throughout the book keeps the reader on the edge of their seat. The plot is complex and well-structured, with many twists and turns that keep the reader guessing until the very end.

Overall, "Verity" is a captivating read that is sure to appeal to fans of psychological thrillers and mystery novels. The book received critical acclaim and was a New York Times bestseller. It has also been optioned for a television series, which speaks to its popularity and potential as a successful story across different mediums.
