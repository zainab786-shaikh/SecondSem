I Will Find You



Author: Harlan Coben
Date of publish: 14 March 2023

ABOUT:
"I Will Find You" was a bestseller and received critical acclaim upon its release in 2017. It was widely praised for its compelling storytelling, insightful commentary on homicide investigations, and Joe Kenda's engaging writing style. The book also gained a significant following due to Kenda's popularity as the host of the true crime television series "Homicide Hunter," which aired on the Investigation Discovery channel. Overall, "I Will Find You" was a popular and well-received book within the true crime genre.

SUMMARY:
"I Will Find You: Solving Killer Cases from My Life Fighting Crime" is a memoir written by Detective Lieutenant Joe Kenda, who spent more than two decades in the Colorado Springs Police Department. The book provides a detailed and captivating account of his career as a homicide detective, during which he investigated over 400 murder cases.

The book is structured into chapters, each of which recounts a specific case that Kenda worked on during his career. These cases range from domestic disputes to complex serial killings, and Kenda shares the details of his investigations in a manner that is both informative and gripping. He describes how he used his intuition, experience, and investigative techniques to solve the cases he worked on.

One of the standout features of the book is Kenda's writing style. His prose is clear, concise, and often humorous, and he conveys the seriousness and emotional intensity of his work while also injecting moments of levity that make the book an enjoyable and entertaining read.

Throughout the book, Kenda emphasizes the importance of following leads, piecing together evidence, and understanding the psychology of the killer in order to solve a case. He also discusses the emotional toll that homicide investigations can take on both the families of the victims and the detectives themselves. He shares stories of cases that affected him personally and provides insight into how he dealt with the emotional stress of his work.

Beyond the cases he worked on, Kenda also reflects on his personal life, discussing how he balanced his demanding career with being a husband and father. He shares insights into his family and personal relationships, as well as his motivations for pursuing a career in law enforcement.

One of the most fascinating aspects of the book is the insight it provides into the inner workings of a homicide investigation. Kenda describes the challenges of piecing together evidence, identifying suspects, and building a case that will stand up in court. He also talks about the importance of understanding the motives and psychology of the killer, and how this knowledge can be used to track them down.

Overall, "I Will Find You" is a compelling and insightful memoir that provides a unique perspective on the world of homicide investigations. Kenda's engaging writing style and fascinating stories make the book an enjoyable read, while also providing a deeper understanding of the complexities and challenges of solving murder cases.
