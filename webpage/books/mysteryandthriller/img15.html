Summary:
Lock Every Door" is a suspenseful thriller novel that follows Jules Larsen, a young woman who is struggling to make ends meet in New York City. When Jules sees an advertisement offering a job as an apartment sitter at the prestigious Bartholomew building, she jumps at the opportunity to live in luxury and earn some much-needed money.

At first, Jules is thrilled to be accepted for the job, especially after seeing the grandeur and lavishness of the Bartholomew. However, as she starts to settle in, she becomes increasingly uneasy about the building's history and her strange neighbors. Her apartment is small and dark, and she hears strange noises in the hallways at night.

Jules befriends another apartment sitter, Ingrid, who has been at the Bartholomew longer than Jules. Ingrid warns Jules about the building's strict rules and regulations and tells her about the mysterious disappearances of several apartment sitters. Despite Ingrid's warnings, Jules is determined to stay and make the most of her luxurious surroundings.

However, Jules' life at the Bartholomew takes a dark turn when Ingrid suddenly disappears. Jules becomes increasingly paranoid and starts to investigate the dark secrets of the Bartholomew and its residents. She starts to uncover a pattern of wealthy and powerful people who use the Bartholomew for their own twisted purposes.

Jules also discovers that the Bartholomew's security staff is watching her every move, and she becomes increasingly aware of their presence. She suspects that they are involved in the disappearance of Ingrid and the other apartment sitters who have gone missing over the years. Jules must navigate through the Bartholomew's dark history and twisted secrets to uncover the truth before it's too late.

As Jules delves deeper into the Bartholomew's history, she discovers that the building has a long and dark past. It was built by a wealthy family with a legacy of violence and tragedy, and it has been home to some of New York's most powerful and corrupt individuals. Jules begins to piece together a web of deceit and corruption that goes beyond the Bartholomew's walls.

As the novel progresses, Jules faces various obstacles and must navigate through complex twists and turns to uncover the truth about the Bartholomew and its residents. She ultimately realizes that she may be the next victim and must fight to escape before it's too late.

"Lock Every Door" is a gripping thriller that explores themes of wealth, power, and the darkness that lurks beneath the surface of luxury. Riley Sager's writing style is engaging and compelling, drawing the reader in and immersing them in the world of the Bartholomew. The novel keeps readers on edge, with unpredictable plot twists and turns that keep them guessing until the very end. It's a thrilling read that will leave you breathless until the last page.
