Ratan Tata



Author: Parimal Kumar
Date of publish: 30 June 2016

ABOUT:
Ratan Tata: The Complete Biography" is a book written by Parimal Kumar. The book tells the story of Ratan Tata, a prominent Indian businessman and philanthropist, who served as the chairman of the Tata Group from 1991 to 2012. The book begins with Tata's family background and childhood, including his education at Cornell University and Harvard Business School. It then traces his early career with the Tata Group, where he held various positions before eventually becoming the chairman.

SUMMARY:
Throughout the book, the author explores Tata's leadership style, business acumen, and his many achievements during his tenure as chairman, including the acquisition of global brands such as Jaguar and Land Rover. The book also highlights Tata's philanthropic work, including his contributions to education, healthcare, and disaster relief. In addition to detailing Tata's professional life, the book also delves into his personal life, including his relationships with his family and friends. It offers insights into his personal philosophy and values, and how these shaped his approach to business and philanthropy.
