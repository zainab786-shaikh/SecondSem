Will



Author: Wiill Smith
Date of publish: 30 June 2016

ABOUT:
"Will" is an autobiography written by Will Smith, the well-known American actor, rapper, and producer. The book provides an inside look into Smith's life and career, from his early days in Philadelphia to his rise to fame in Hollywood. In the book, Smith shares his personal journey, including his struggles with poverty, family issues, and his eventual success in the entertainment industry. He discusses his experiences as a rapper, his transition to acting, and the challenges he faced in building a career in Hollywood.

SUMMARY:
The book also provides insights into Smith's personal life, including his relationships with his family and friends, his marriage to Jada Pinkett Smith, and his experiences as a father to his three children. Throughout the book, Smith emphasizes the importance of hard work, determination, and staying true to oneself in the pursuit of success. He also shares valuable lessons learned from his failures and setbacks, as well as his triumphs and achievements. Overall, "Will" is an engaging and inspiring autobiography that offers a unique perspective on the life of one of Hollywood's most beloved actors. It is a must-read for fans of Smith and anyone seeking motivation and inspiration in their own pursuits.
