Usain Bolt: Faster Than Lightning



Author: Usain Bolt
Date of publish: 30 June 2016

ABOUT:
"Usain Bolt: Faster Than Lightning" is an autobiography written by Usain Bolt, one of the greatest sprinters of all time. The book provides an inside look into Bolt's life and career, from his early days in Jamaica to his record-breaking performances on the world stage.

SUMMARY:
The book details Bolt's journey from a young boy with a talent for running to becoming a three-time Olympic gold medalist and a world record holder. It explores his training regimen, his mental approach to racing, and the support of his family and coaches. Bolt also shares personal stories from his life, including his love of music and partying, and his relationships with his teammates and competitors. He also discusses his experiences with injury and setbacks, and how he overcame them to achieve his goals. In addition to Bolt's personal story, the book also provides a behind-the-scenes look into the world of elite sprinting, including the training, competition, and rivalries that define the sport.
