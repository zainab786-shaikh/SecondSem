Messi



Author: Guillem Balague
Date of publish: 30 June 2016

ABOUT:
The book "Messi" by Guillem Balague is a comprehensive biography of the Argentine footballer Lionel Messi, one of the greatest players of all time. The book details Messi's life from his humble beginnings in Rosario, Argentina to his ascent to global stardom. The book offers insight into Messi's upbringing in a football-obsessed family and his early years at Barcelona's La Masia youth academy. It also examines Messi's playing style, his strengths and weaknesses on the pitch, and his remarkable ability to read the game and make split-second decisions. Balague also delves into Messi's personal life, exploring the relationships with his family, friends, and teammates, and how they have shaped him as a person and a player. The book also chronicles Messi's struggles with injuries, the pressure of international competition, and his complex relationship with Argentina's national team.

SUMMARY:
Balague provides an in-depth analysis of Messi's career and his impact on the sport of football, as well as his legacy as a role model and inspiration for future generations. Overall, "Messi" is an insightful and engaging biography that offers a fascinating glimpse into the life and career of one of football's most iconic figures. It is a must-read for fans of Messi and football alike, providing a deeper understanding of the man behind the incredible talent.
