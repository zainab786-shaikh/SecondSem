Journey to the Center of the Earth



Author: Jules Verne
Date of publish: 25 november 1864

ABOUT:
"Journey to the Center of the Earth" is a classic science fiction adventure novel by Jules Verne, which follows a geology professor and his nephew who embark on a perilous journey to the center of the earth after discovering a coded message in an old book. Along the way, they encounter obstacles and dangers, strange and fantastical landscapes, and eventually reach the center of the earth, where they discover a mysterious and wondrous subterranean world. The book is a thrilling and imaginative adventure story that captures the excitement and wonder of exploration and discovery.

SUMMARY:

"Journey to the Center of the Earth" is a classic science fiction adventure novel by Jules Verne, published in 1864. The book tells the story of a geology professor named Otto Lidenbrock and his nephew, Axel, who embark on a perilous journey to the center of the earth after discovering a coded message in an old book.

Lidenbrock is a brilliant but eccentric professor of geology who becomes obsessed with the idea of traveling to the center of the earth. He deciphers a message in an old manuscript that leads him to believe that a volcanic tube in Iceland will take them to their destination.

Lidenbrock and Axel set out on their journey with a local guide, Hans Bjelke, and face many obstacles and dangers along the way. They must navigate treacherous paths, cross underground rivers, and avoid prehistoric creatures that have survived in the subterranean world.

As they journey deeper into the earth, Lidenbrock and Axel encounter strange and fantastical landscapes, including vast underground oceans and forests filled with giant mushrooms and lizards. They also discover a primitive human society ruled by a powerful queen.

The journey becomes increasingly perilous as the trio faces earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, and other natural disasters. Eventually, they reach the center of the earth, where they discover a strange and mysterious subterranean world, filled with wonders and terrors beyond their wildest imagination.

"Journey to the Center of the Earth" is a thrilling and imaginative adventure story that captures the excitement and wonder of exploration and discovery. The book has been praised for its vivid and detailed descriptions of the subterranean world, as well as its engaging characters and themes of scientific inquiry and adventure. It has been adapted into numerous films and television shows, and remains a beloved classic of the science fiction genre.
